About Us

Eruviv Nigeria Limited(RC:8224729) is an indiginous company dedicated to provide Occupational Health,Safety, Security and Environmental consultancy service to Individuals, Organizations, Corporate Institutions and Government Agencies. We provide quality HSSE training, supply internationally approved Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), Safety and Security Equipment and conduct Research & Development services for our clients.

    • Founder's Profile

    Dr. Iniovoua, Erute is a graduate of Industrial Safety and Environmental Engineering Technology.He also bagged a Bachelor of Technology;MBA in Business Administration and Doctorate degree in Economic Policy Management, with specialty in Policy Management. His wealth of field experience spans over 17 years in the corporate organizations; conducting safety audit, security assessment and implementing QHSSE policies with an award as “the Best HSSE Staff of 2024”

    He is a Certified Management Consultant; Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants of Nigeria;Recognized Safety Practitioner and Associate of the Institute of Safety Professionals of Nigeria (ISPON). He has attended several Safety and Security training organized by the Federal Fire Service, Department of State Services (DSS) and other world class institutions. He has served in the Managerial level at the Central Bank of Nigeria, where he was the Head Fire, Safety and Environment Unit and also Deputy Manager, Security Operations until his voluntary retirement in 2024 to pursue his passion of consultancy services and Research & Development.
